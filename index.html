Beetroot (Beta vulgaris)

  1. Common varieties

55 to 65 days are needed from permination to harvest of the root.

The usual deep purple of beetroot are eaten either grilled, boiled, or roasted as a cooked vegetable, cold as a salad after cooking and adding oil and vinegar, or raw and shredded, either alone or combined with any salad vegetable.
